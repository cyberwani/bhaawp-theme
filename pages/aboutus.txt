[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>What is the BHAA</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]<p>The Business Houses Athletic Association is a work place athletic organisation who's aim is to facilitate colleagues to run together in a series of races.</p>[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>What do you do</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]<p>We organise a series of properly managed cross country, trail and road races over the year.<p>[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>When did it start</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]The BHAA was founded in 1979 with an inter firm race in Swords. We now have branches in Dublin and Cork[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>Where are your races</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]Our races are based around Dublin city and its surrounds.[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>Are their prizes</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]There are prizes for the top three in each and also for each the age categories. There are also 15 prizes at each event[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>Tell me about Teams and Companies</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]Once you have three people from the same company that forms a team.[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>So how do I join</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]New members can register online for annual membership for 15e, and each race then costs 10e. People who only want to run one race can register as a day member for an event for 15e. See the <a href="./prices/" title="Prices">Prices</a> page for all options.[/three_fourth]
[one_fourth last="no"]<h3>What is a standard</h3>[/one_fourth][three_fourth last="yes"]Standards are a bit like a golf handicap with a better time resulting in a decrease in your standard and a less than par performance resulting in upping a standard. You get a standard after your first race.[/three_fourth]
